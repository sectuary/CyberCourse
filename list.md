# Cybersecurity Certifications Sorted by Skill Level & Domain

## **Penetration Testing (General)**

### **Beginner Level**

1.  **[eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester (eJPT)](https://www.ine.com/security/ejpt-certification.html)**
    - **Price:** **$199** (Exam voucher only). Often bundled with training.
    - **Focus**: Foundational penetration testing, networking, and security tools.
    - **Registration**: Purchase on the INE website.

2.  **[Practical Junior Penetration Tester (PJPT)](https://www.tcm-sec.com/pjpt/)**
    - **Price:** **$199**
    - **Focus**: Practical, hands-on introductory pentesting skills.
    - **Registration**: Sign up and purchase on the TCM Security website.

### **Intermediate Level**

1.  **[GIAC Penetration Tester (GPEN)](https://www.giac.org/certifications/penetration-tester-gpen/)**
    - **Price:** **$979** (Exam). SANS training is separate and costly.
    - **Focus**: Network penetration testing, exploitation, and vulnerability management.
    - **Registration**: Register through the GIAC portal.

2.  **[Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP)](https://www.offsec.com/courses/pen-200/)**
    - **Price:** **$1,649** (Course + 1 exam attempt).
    - **Focus**: Hands-on penetration testing and exploitation in a controlled lab.
    - **Registration**: "Buy Now" on Offensive Security's website.

3.  **[eLearnSecurity Certified Professional Penetration Tester (eCPPT)](https://www.ine.com/security/ecppt-certification.html)**
    - **Price:** **$600** (Exam voucher). Bundled with training.
    - **Focus**: Comprehensive penetration testing, including pivoting and writing reports.
    - **Registration**: Purchase on the INE website.

4.  **[Certified Penetration Testing Professional (CPENT)](https://www.eccouncil.org/programs/certified-penetration-testing-professional-cpent/)**
    - **Price:** **$550** (Exam voucher). Training is extra.
    - **Focus**: Penetration testing in enterprise, OT, and IoT environments.
    - **Registration**: Purchase through EC-Council.

5.  **[Practical Network Penetration Tester (PNPT)](https://www.tcm-sec.com/pnpt/)**
    - **Price:** **$399**
    - **Focus**: Practical exam focused on offensive security and Active Directory.
    - **Registration**: Sign up and purchase on the TCM Security website.

6.  **[Hack The Box Certified Penetration Testing Specialist (HTB CPTS)](https://academy.hackthebox.com/certifications/cpts)**
    - **Price:** **$210** (One-time exam attempt) or **$8/month** (Subscription with free attempt).
    - **Focus**: Comprehensive penetration testing across various domains.
    - **Registration**: Access through Hack The Box Academy.

### **Advanced Level**

1.  **[Offensive Security Experienced Penetration Tester (OSEP)](https://www.offsec.com/courses/pen-300/)**
    - **Price:** **$1,799** (Course + 1 exam attempt).
    - **Focus**: Advanced evasion techniques, lateral movement, and bypassing defenses.
    - **Registration**: "Buy Now" on Offensive Security's website.

2.  **[Offensive Security Certified Expert (OSCE)](https://www.offsec.com/courses/pen-210/)**
    - **Price:** **$1,499** (Course + 1 exam attempt).
    - **Focus**: Advanced penetration testing and custom exploit development.
    - **Registration**: "Buy Now" on Offensive Security's website. (Note: Often considered a step between OSCP and OSCE3).

3.  **[eLearnSecurity Certified Penetration Tester eXtreme (eCPTXv2)](https://www.ine.com/security/ecptx-certification.html)**
    - **Price:** **$700** (Exam voucher). Bundled with training.
    - **Focus**: Advanced penetration testing against complex enterprise networks.
    - **Registration**: Purchase on the INE website.

4.  **[Offensive Security Certified Expert 3 (OSCE3)](https://www.offsec.com/certifications/osse3/)**
    - **Price:** **N/A** (Achieved by earning OSCP, OSEP, and OSWE).
    - **Focus**: Mastery of penetration testing across systems, networks, and web apps.
    - **Registration**: Earn the three component certifications.
